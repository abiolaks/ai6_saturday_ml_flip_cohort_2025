Insights That Matter

  • 6 numeric features show ≥5% outliers. Consider trimming/winsorization or robust scalers. Notable: cafes, nightlife, lot_size, shopping, restaurants....
  • Top numeric correlations: tx_price–basement: nan; beds–sqft: 0.69; beds–basement: nan.
  • Key drivers of tx_price: insurance, property_tax, lot_size, sqft, married.
  • Median tx_price is up ~19.3% over the last 3 months vs prior 3.

Recommended Actions

  1. Use tx_price drivers (top features) to refine pricing models and agent playbooks.
  2. Time promotions with months showing rising prices and higher volume.
  3. Monitor multicollinearity in linear models; prefer trees or regularization if needed.
  4. Fix high-missing fields at collection; add validation and mandatory capture for critical attributes.

Notes

The analysis is exploratory. Validate with out-of-sample data and perform stability tests across time and sub-markets before production use.
